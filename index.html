This is "memorize" or "match images" game. In "easy" mode, you click cards to flip them, and see the images. If two flipped images match, they will remain open, and you can open more images. If two images don't match - remember the cats, and click any of the cards, the "not matched" images will be turned back. The goal is to open all images.

In the "normal" mode, there is 3 copies of each cat; you need to open three similar images in a row, instead of two.

In the "insane" mode (not implemented yet), you will find hundreds of cats, and 10 copies of each. Do not click this button.
